Smooth curves

This page will draw a smooth curve between several points on a graph.

The graph already has three points on it. Move them around, or add new ones, and see how the curve changes in response.

Instructions:

  • Click on the canvas to add points.
  • Click and drag a point to move it.
  • Drag a point outside the graph or shift-click to delete it.

The graph is calculated with cubic interpolation. This makes sure that the graph is differentiable (no sharp corners) everywhere.
